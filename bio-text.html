"Separated by the Ocean"

by Elizabeth Fernandez

Santo Domingo and New York City.
Separated by 1500 plus miles.
One a tropical paradise, the other a place where you can fulfill your dreams.
Separated by an Ocean.
One the place I call home, the other the place where I reside.
Separated by the Caribbean Sea.
One where I was born, the other where I grew up.
Separated by the different skies and clouds.
One a part of my memory, the other a part of my present.
Santo Domingo and New York City.
